# EvoViewer

**ID:** simianevo-evo-viewer

**Project:** [Other/Evo Viewer](https://sec.lumc.nl/mtg-viewer/MV_data/CytosploreHMBAViewer/EvoViewer/EvoViewerSubsetProject.mv) in the [Cytosplore Viewer](/#get)

**Contact:** Soumyadeep
**Email:** s.basu@lumc.nl

**Description:** Cytosplore EvoViewer enables interactive exploration of single-cell transcriptomic datasets across species. It links marker gene expression with comparative evolutionary information, allowing users to detect subtle similarities and differences in gene regulation and cellular composition. Users can analyze cross-species relationships, compare evolutionary traits, and investigate the impact of marker genes on biological processes.
