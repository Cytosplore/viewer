# Middle Temporal Gyrus Viewer

**ID:** classic-mtg-viewer

![Classic MTG Screenshot](/assets/papers/ClassicViewers/mtg.png)

**Project:** MTG in the [Classic Viewer](/documentation/releases#classic-viewer-install)

**Contact:** Jeroen
**Email:** j.eggermont@lumc.nl

**Description:** Interactive viewer of human middle temporal gyrus single‑nucleus transcriptomics. Shows a diverse atlas of excitatory and inhibitory neuron types in human MTG, highlights conservation with mouse enabling homologous matches, and exposes species‑specific differences in proportions, laminar distribution, gene expression, and morphology.

- [Publication](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-019-1506-7)
