**Project:** [HMBA:Basal Ganglia Preprint/Spatial Transcriptomics Multi-species Basal Ganglia](https://allen-hmba-releases.s3.amazonaws.com/cytosplore/DeploymentTesting/Preprint_2025_Spatial_Multispecies_BG.mv) in the [Cytosplore Viewer](/#get)

**Contact:** Soumyadeep
**Email:** s.basu@lumc.nl

**Description:** This viewer allows interactive analysis of spatial transcriptomics data, linking tissue coordinates with gene expression gradients. Users can explore spatial directions, identify genes with aligned expression patterns across datasets, and compare gradients even when datasets share only a subset of genes, facilitating cross-dataset and cross-modality analyses.
