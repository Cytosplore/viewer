# Middle Temporal Gyrus Viewer

**ID:** classic-viewer

![Cortex screenshot](/assets/papers/Classic_Viewer.gif)

**Project:** the relevant projects in the [Classic Viewer](/documentation/releases#classic-viewer-install)

**Contact:** Jeroen
**Email:** j.eggermont@lumc.nl

**Description:** The Classic Viewer enables interactive exploration of transcriptomic and epigenomic data from human, marmoset, and mouse primary motor cortex. It links cross-species cell-type classifications with gene expression and epigenetic states, allowing users to analyze conserved and species-specific features and specialized neuronal populations.
