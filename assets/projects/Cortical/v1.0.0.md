# Middle Temporal Gyrus Viewer

![Cortex screenshot](/assets/papers/MTG_Viewer.gif)

**Project:** the relevant projects in the [Classic Viewer](/#get)

**Contact:** Jeroen (j[dot]eggermont[at]lumc[dot]nl)

**Description:** The MTG Viewer enables interactive exploration of transcriptomic and epigenomic data from human, marmoset, and mouse primary motor cortex. It links cross-species cell-type classifications with gene expression and epigenetic states, allowing users to analyze conserved and species-specific features and specialized neuronal populations.
