Cytosplore Viewer is being developed by a team consisting of members from the Leiden Computational Biology Center, the Division of Image Processing at the Leiden University Medical Center, and the Computer Graphics and Visualization Group at the TU Delft in a research project, carried out in close collaboration with Trygve Bakken, Jeremy Miller, Brian Long, Rebecca Hodge, Bosiljka Tasic, Zizhen Yao, Lucas T. Graybuck, Hongkui Zeng, Mike Hawrylycz , and Ed Lein from the Allen Institute for Brain Science.

This work was partially supported by an NWO Gravitation grant:BRAINSCAPES: A Roadmap from Neurogenetics to Neurobiology (NWO: 024.004.012) and NWO AES grants 3DOMICS (NWO: 17126), VANPIRE (NWO: 12720) and Genes in Space (NWO: 12721).
